---
title: "Registration Requirements"
description: "Configure what players must meet before their registration is complete."
---

When creating a tournament or scrim, you can toggle up to four requirements that players must satisfy to register. These apply regardless of your registration mode.

## The 4 Requirements

### 1. Registering User Must Join Club

The player submitting the registration must be a member of your club. If they're not already a member, they'll be prompted to join your club during the registration process. This only applies to the player who is registering the team — not all team members.

### 2. Require Full Roster

The team must have the minimum number of members filled based on the game mode:

- **Solo** — 1 member
- **Duo** — 2 members
- **Squad** — 4 members (up to 6 with substitutes)

The registration won't be considered complete until the roster meets this requirement.

### 3. Require All Members in Club

Every team member must be registered on the platform AND be a member of your club. This automatically enables the first two requirements (registering user must join club + full roster).

The registering player is prompted to join the club at the time of registration. Other team members will see a sticky banner on the status page telling them to join the club, along with details on which members still need to join.

### 4. Require UID/IGN

Players must provide their Free Fire User ID and In-Game Name for this tournament. This automatically enables the full roster requirement.

This is set per tournament — players enter their UID and IGN during the registration process for each event.

## How Requirements Work Together

Some requirements auto-enable others:

- **Require all members in club** → auto-enables Registering user must join club + Require full roster
- **Require UID/IGN** → auto-enables Require full roster

## What Players See

Players see all requirements listed on the registration page before they register. After registering, the status page shows:

- Their registration status
- Any pending requirements — which members still need to join the club, missing roster spots, etc.
- A sticky banner at the top if action is needed

## When to Use What

| Scenario | Recommended Requirements |
|----------|------------------------|
| Casual weekly scrim | None — keep it frictionless |
| Community tournament | Registering user must join club |
| Competitive tournament | Require full roster + Require UID/IGN |
| Serious invitational | All four — full control |
