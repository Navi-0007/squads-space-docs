---

Club Settings lets you shape how your club appears to players across the platform. Access it from the Organizer sidebar.

## Club Identity

- **Club name** — your club's display name. Appears on your public club page, tournament cards, and everywhere your club is referenced.
- **Region** — your country/region. Helps players find local organizers.

## Social Links

Add social links so players can connect with your community outside of SquadsSpace:

- **Discord** — your server invite link
- **YouTube** — your channel URL
- **WhatsApp** — your group or community link

Social links appear on your public club page and in the Organizer Dashboard. YouTube links support verification — the system confirms the channel exists and pulls display info.

## Club Banner

Upload or choose a banner image that appears at the top of your public club page. A good banner helps your club stand out when players are browsing.
