SquadsSpace is a tournament platform built for Free Fire in India. Whether you're competing in scrims and tournaments or organizing them, everything lives in one place — registrations, scores, leaderboards, room credentials, and stream overlays.

## For Players

SquadsSpace is where you find and join Free Fire tournaments and scrims. No more scrolling through WhatsApp groups for room IDs or asking "bhai score kab aayega?"

What you can do:

- **Browse tournaments and scrims** — discover events, check formats, and see what's open
- **Register your team** — fill in your details, track your registration status, and get confirmed
- **Get room ID & password** — see your match credentials right on the platform, no Discord hunting
- **View match schedules** — know exactly when your matches are happening
- **Turn on email notifications** — get notified when it matters. You can enable notifications for a specific match, an entire group, or the whole tournament — your choice
- **View live leaderboards** — watch standings update in real-time as scores are entered
- **Check your match history** — see your past results across events

**Coming soon:** Player stats tracking and a tiering system.

## For Organizers

SquadsSpace replaces the Excel sheets, calculators, and manual work you're doing right now. Your community stays on WhatsApp and Discord — SquadsSpace is the engine behind the scenes.

What you can do:

- **Create tournaments and scrims** with custom formats, point tables, and registration settings
- **Enter match scores** and get instant point calculations — no more manual math
- **Build multi-stage roadmaps** — qualifiers → semifinals → finals with elimination and round robin formats
- **Manage registrations** — 4 different modes from open to invite-only
- **Stream with live overlays** — broadcast-ready scoreboards that update in real-time
- **Auto-generate slot lists** for the next round based on results
- **Track per-player kills** tied to verified Free Fire UIDs

## Getting Started

**Players:** Head to [platform.squads-space.com](https://platform.squads-space.com/auth) and sign up with your email. You can browse tournaments immediately.

**Organizers:** Sign up as a player first, then convert to an organizer at [/organizer/register](https://platform.squads-space.com/organizer/register). You keep all player abilities plus full tournament management tools.

**New here?** Check the [Quick Start Guide](/getting-started/quick-start) to get going in 5 minutes.
