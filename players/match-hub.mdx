Once you're confirmed and assigned to a group, the Match Hub is where you follow your tournament matches. Access it from the tournament page — a "Matches" link appears when you're an active participant.

The Match Hub has four tabs (three for scrims — Roadmap is excluded).

## Matches

Your main match view. Shows:

- **State card** — your current group assignment, stage, and status at a glance
- **Match selector** — switch between matches (Match 1, Match 2, etc.)
- **Schedule** — when the selected match starts, its current status, and the map
- **Room ID & Password** — the lobby credentials. These appear when the match is started by the organizer. Copy them to join the in-game lobby.
- **Announcements** — messages from the organizer for your group

You can also view the slot list to see all teams in your group with their details.

If you haven't been assigned to a group yet, you'll see a waiting state explaining that group assignment is pending.

## Roadmap

Shows the full tournament structure — all stages, which stage you're currently in, and how advancement works. For scrims, this tab doesn't appear since scrims have no multi-stage structure.

If the organizer hasn't set up the roadmap yet, you'll see a message that it's coming.

## Standings

The cumulative leaderboard for your group across all completed matches. Shows rank, team name, total points, kills, kill points, and placement points.

Teams above the qualifier line (set by the organizer's roadmap) are highlighted — so you can see at a glance whether your team is advancing.

Standings appear after the first match has results.

## Live Scoring

Real-time results as the organizer enters scores during a live stream. This tab has three view modes:

- **Live Stream** — if the organizer is running a live scoring session right now, you see standings update in real-time as scores are entered. The connection status shows whether you're receiving live updates.
- **Final Standings** — the cumulative standings (same as the Standings tab)
- **Match snapshots** — select any individual match to see its specific results

The view automatically switches to the most relevant mode — live when a session is active, final standings when matches are done.
