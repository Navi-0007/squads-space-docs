After registering for a tournament, the Registration Status page is your home base for that event. Access it from My Tournaments or from the tournament page itself.

## Status Overview

Your registration status shows as one of:

- **Pending** — waiting for organizer approval
- **Confirmed** — you're in, requirements may still need completing
- **Rejected** — the organizer declined your registration

## Requirements Progress

If the tournament has requirements enabled, you'll see a progress tracker showing what's complete and what still needs attention:

- **Club membership** — join the organizer's club. A Join Club button appears if you haven't yet.
- **Full roster** — your team needs all member slots filled. Share the registration invite link with teammates.
- **All members in club** — every team member must join the organizer's club.
- **UID/IGN verification** — provide your in-game User ID and In-Game Name. A Provide UID button opens the form.

Each requirement updates in real-time as conditions are met.

## Managing Your Team

Depending on the tournament format and how you registered:

- **Manage Squad** — adjust your team composition (if using Custom Squad registration)
- **Invite teammates** — share the registration link so teammates can join your team
- **Leave Squad** — leave a custom squad you joined
- **Cancel Registration** — for Solo tournaments, cancel your own registration

## What Happens Next

The status page shows context-aware next steps:

- If you need to invite more members, it tells you how many spots remain
- If you're waiting for approval, it lets you know
- If you need to provide your UID, it prompts you
- Once everything is complete, it confirms you're ready for match day

## Alerts

The page shows alerts for important situations — like if the tournament has been paused, cancelled, or if registration is about to close.
