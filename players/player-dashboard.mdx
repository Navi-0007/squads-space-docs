The Dashboard is the first thing you see after signing in. It gives you a snapshot of everything happening.

## Quick Actions

Shortcut buttons for the most common tasks — browse tournaments, manage your squad, and more.

## My Tournaments

Shows your active tournament registrations. Click any card to go to that tournament's Match Hub or Registration Status page.

## Clubs

If you've joined any clubs, they appear here. If you haven't joined any yet, you'll see a list of top clubs on the platform to explore.

## Recommended Tournaments

Tournaments you might be interested in based on what's currently open for registration.

## Organizer Events

If you're also an organizer, a dedicated section shows the tournaments you manage — keeping your player and organizer activities separate but accessible from the same dashboard.

## Profile & Settings

From the navigation, access your Profile to update your display name, avatar, and other details. Settings lets you manage your account preferences.
