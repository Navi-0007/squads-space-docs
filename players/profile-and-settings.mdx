## Profile

Your profile is your public identity on SquadsSpace. Access it from the navigation.

Your profile page shows:

- **Hero banner** — your display name, username, and avatar
- **Player details** — your basic information
- **Tournaments** — tournaments you've participated in
- **Social links** — connected social accounts
- **Clubs** — clubs you've joined
- **Player Stats** — performance statistics (coming soon)

## Settings

Settings gives you control over your account. It has six tabs:

### General

A quick-start checklist with shortcuts to the most important setup steps — completing your profile, securing your account, and reviewing active sessions.

### Account

Manage your core account details — display name, email, and other identity information.

### Notifications

Control which notifications you receive and how you receive them.

### Security

Password management and account security options.

### Sessions

View and manage your active login sessions. See where you're signed in and revoke access if needed.

### Privacy

Control your privacy preferences and data visibility.
