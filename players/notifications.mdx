The Notifications page collects all your platform activity in one place. Access it from the bell icon in the navigation.

## Notification Tabs

- **All** — every notification you've received
- **Unread** — only notifications you haven't opened yet
- **Saved** — notifications you've saved for later

## What Gets Notified

You'll receive notifications for events like:

- Tournament registration confirmations and status changes
- Squad invites and membership updates
- Organizer announcements for your groups
- Match schedule updates and room credential availability
- Stage advancement and tournament results

## Actions

- **Mark all as read** — clear all unread notifications at once
- **Open** — click any notification to navigate directly to the relevant page
- Notifications are automatically marked as seen when they scroll into view
