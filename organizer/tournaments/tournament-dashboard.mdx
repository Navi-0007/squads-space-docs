The Tournament Dashboard is where you manage everything about a specific tournament. It opens when you click on a tournament from your Organizer Dashboard.

## Dashboard Tabs

The dashboard has five tabs:

### Overview

Shows the tournament's current status, key stats (registrations, capacity), and quick actions. From here you can publish, share, access status control, and navigate to stages.

For completed, cancelled, or paused tournaments, the overview shows a dedicated view with the relevant information and available actions (like resuming a paused tournament).

### Roadmap

View your tournament's stage structure. If you haven't created a roadmap yet, you'll see a prompt to configure one. Once created, each stage card links to the stage management page. You can also download the roadmap as an image or PDF.

### Registrations

View all registrations with filtering (All, Pending, Confirmed, Rejected), search, and bulk actions. See [Reviewing Registrations](/organizer/managing-players/reviewing-registrations) for details.

### Game Settings

Shows your tournament's scoring configuration at a glance — points per kill, placement points table, tiebreaker priority, allowed platforms (Android, iOS, PC), and team size limits. These are set during tournament creation.

### Analytics (Coming Soon)

Registration progress, confirmed count, and capacity metrics. Charts for registration timeline, stage progression, and performance metrics will be available in a future update.

## Sharing

From the dashboard header, you can open the Share modal to get your tournament's public link and server invite link for distribution.
