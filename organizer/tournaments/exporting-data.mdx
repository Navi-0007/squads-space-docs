title: "Exporting Tournament Data"
description: "Export tournament data as Excel spreadsheets or PDF flyers."
---

Export lets you download tournament data for reporting, sharing, or record-keeping. Access it from the Tournaments list in your Organizer Dashboard.

## Export Flow

The export wizard has three steps:

### 1. Choose Categories & Format

Filter which tournaments to include by category:

- **Upcoming** — published and draft tournaments not yet started
- **Registration Open** — tournaments currently accepting registrations
- **Ongoing** — in progress, registration closed, or paused
- **Completed** — finished or cancelled tournaments

Choose your export format:

- **Excel Spreadsheet** (.xlsx) — full data with all columns
- **PDF Flyer** — branded flyer for sharing

### 2. Select Tournaments

Browse and select which specific tournaments to include in the export. You can select all or pick individually.

### 3. Review & Export

Review your selections and download the file.
