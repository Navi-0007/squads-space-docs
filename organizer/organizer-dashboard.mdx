The Organizer Dashboard is the first screen you see in the organizer view. Access it from the sidebar or by navigating to the Organizer section.

## Club Details

Shows your club's name, region, member count, and a quick snapshot of your identity on the platform. This is the same information players see on your public club page.

## Quick Actions

Shortcut cards to the most common tasks — create a new tournament, manage club settings, view members, and start a live scoring session.

## Tournaments

A list of your tournaments showing current status, format, and registration stats. Click any tournament to go directly to its Tournament Dashboard.

## Social Links

Shows the social links you've configured for your club — Discord, YouTube, and WhatsApp. These appear on your public club page and help players connect with your community.

## Sidebar Navigation

From any organizer page, the sidebar gives you quick access to:

- **Dashboard** — this page
- **Club Settings** — edit club name, region, social links, and banner
- **Members** — view and manage club members
- **Live Scoring** — standalone scoring sessions
- **Tournaments** — full tournament list with creation and management
