SquadsSpace generates overlay URLs that you can add as browser sources in OBS or any streaming software. They update in real-time as you enter scores — no manual graphic updates needed.

## Overlay Types

When you start a live scoring session, the platform generates three overlay URLs:

### Overall Standings (Side Overlay)

A compact leaderboard that updates in real-time as you enter scores. This is your main overlay during gameplay — add it as a browser source on the side of your stream. Uses the standings template you selected during broadcast setup.

### Per-Match Results

Individual overlay URLs for each match (Match 1, Match 2, etc.). Shows placements, kills, and points for that specific match only. Use these to display results right after a match ends.

### Post-Match 16:9 Overlay

A full-screen 16:9 overlay designed for broadcast-quality post-match displays. Uses the wide template you selected during broadcast setup. Perfect for dedicated results screens between matches — matches the look of professional Free Fire broadcasts like FFIC and FFWS.

All overlay URLs also work for standalone live scoring sessions.

## Broadcast Configuration

Before starting a live scoring session, you can configure your broadcast display:

- **Tournament name** — displayed on the overlay header
- **Stage name** — shown alongside the tournament name
- **Labels** — customize the "Overall" and "Match" labels
- **Instagram handle** — displayed on overlays for branding
- **Discord handle** — displayed on overlays for community promotion
- **Template selection** — choose from available templates for your overlays

## Adding to OBS

1. Start a live scoring session from your group or pairing page
2. Copy the overlay URL you want to use
3. In OBS, add a new Browser Source
4. Paste the URL
5. Set the width and height to match your stream layout (1920x1080 for 16:9 overlays)

The overlay will automatically update as you enter scores during live scoring — no need to refresh or reconfigure.

## Template Gallery

SquadsSpace includes 21 broadcast-quality overlay templates in two categories:

**Side overlays** (for live standings during matches) — 11 templates including Apex GFX, Apex Ice, Apex Crimson, Tactical Red, Tactical Amber, Tactical Cyan, Tactical Green, Squads Board, Luxury Gold, Luxury Crimson, Luxury Ice, Swiss Editorial, Swiss Carbon, and Swiss Cobalt.

**Wide 16:9 overlays** (for post-match full-screen displays) — 7 templates including Luxury Gold Wide, Luxury Crimson Wide, Luxury Ice Wide, Luxury Asymmetric, Swiss Editorial Wide, Swiss Carbon Wide, and Swiss Cobalt Wide.

You choose separate templates for your live standings and post-match overlays during broadcast setup. Browse the template gallery to preview each one before selecting.
