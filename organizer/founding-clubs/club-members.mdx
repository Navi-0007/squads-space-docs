The Members page shows everyone who has joined your club. Access it from the Organizer sidebar under Members.

## What You Can See

For each member, you'll see their display name, username, and when they joined. The member list gives you an overview of your community size and growth.

## How Members Join

Players join your club in several ways:

- Browsing and joining from your public club page
- Being prompted to join during tournament registration (when "Registering user must join club" is enabled)
- Seeing the join requirement on a tournament status page (when "Require all members in club" is enabled)

## Growing Your Community

Every tournament you run with club membership requirements brings new members into your club. Players who register for your tournaments become part of your community, making it easier to reach them for future events.
