---

The Stage Summary gives you a bird's-eye view of an entire stage — standings across all groups, qualifier status, and match completion.

## Summary Tabs

### Overview

Quick stats for the stage: total teams, groups/pairings count, match completion progress, and top standings at a glance.

### Qualifiers

Shows which teams have qualified (or will qualify) from each group based on the roadmap's advancement settings. For Elimination stages, qualifiers are shown per group. For Round Robin stages, qualifiers come from the combined leaderboard.

### Standings

Full standings for every group in the stage. Shows team rankings with points, kills, and placement breakdowns.

### Matches

Summary of all matches across the stage — how many are completed, scheduled, in progress, or cancelled.

## Accessing Stage Summary

From the stage management page, click Summary to open the stage summary view.
