## All Tournaments

The All Tournaments page shows every public tournament and scrim on SquadsSpace. You can switch between grid and list views.

### Filters

Narrow down tournaments using:

- **Search** — find by name
- **Format** — Solo, Duo, or Squad
- **Mode** — Battle Royale
- **Status** — Upcoming (published but not yet open) or Open (currently accepting registrations)
- **Sort** — Relevance, Newest, or Starting Soon

### Tournament Cards

Each card shows the tournament name, organizer club, format, status, poster image, and registration progress (how many slots are filled vs total capacity).

## My Tournaments

Once you've registered for tournaments, they appear in My Tournaments. This page shows all your active and past registrations with filters to find specific ones.

## Club Pages

You can also discover tournaments through club pages. Every organizer has a public club page showing all their events. Browse clubs from the Clubs directory.
