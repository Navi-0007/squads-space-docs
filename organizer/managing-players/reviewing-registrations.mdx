The registrations page is your central hub for managing who's in your tournament. You can review individual registrations, take bulk actions, and track requirement completion.

## Accessing Registrations

From your tournament's dashboard, open the Registrations tab. You'll see stats at the top showing total, pending, confirmed, and rejected counts.

## Filtering and Searching

Filter registrations by status:

- **All** — every registration
- **Pending** — waiting for your approval (Approval Required and Invite Only with Approval modes)
- **Confirmed** — approved and counted toward slots
- **Rejected** — registrations you've rejected

You can also search by name, squad name, or ID to find specific registrations quickly.

## Reviewing Individual Registrations

Click on any registration to open a detail panel showing:

- **Team information** — squad name, captain, registration date
- **Members list** — all team members with their details
- **Requirement progress** — which requirements are met and which are still pending (club membership, full roster, UID/IGN)
- **Status** — current registration status

From the detail panel, you can approve, reject, or delete individual registrations.

## Bulk Actions

Select multiple registrations to take action in bulk:

- **Bulk Approve** — confirm all selected registrations at once
- **Bulk Reject** — reject all selected registrations
- **Bulk Delete** — permanently remove selected registrations

Bulk actions save time when you have many pending registrations to process — especially for large tournaments with Approval Required mode.

## Registration Statuses

- **Pending** — submitted but awaiting approval. Only applies to Approval Required and Invite Only with Approval modes.
- **Confirmed** — approved (or auto-confirmed for Open/Invite Only modes). Counts toward your slot limit.
- **Rejected** — registration was denied by the organizer.

## Tips

- For Open registration tournaments, registrations are auto-confirmed — you don't need to manually approve each one
- Keep an eye on requirement progress — even confirmed teams may still have pending requirements like club membership for all members
- Use bulk actions to process large batches quickly during registration close
