Announcements let you communicate with players in a specific group or pairing. Players see your announcements in the Match Hub alongside their match details.

## Creating an Announcement

From a group or pairing page, navigate to Announcements. Click New Announcement and fill in:

- **Title** — a short headline
- **Description** — the full message
- **Type** — choose from Info, Warning, Success, or Alert. The type controls the visual styling so players immediately understand the urgency.

## Announcement Types

- **Info** — general updates like schedule reminders or format clarifications
- **Warning** — important notices like delays or rule changes
- **Success** — positive news like confirmed schedules or results posted
- **Alert** — urgent messages like emergency reschedules or disqualifications

## Managing Announcements

The announcements page shows all announcements for that group with search and type filtering. You can edit existing announcements or create new ones.

## Player View

Players see announcements in the Announcements card within their Match Hub. The card shows the latest announcements for their assigned group, with a count of total announcements.
