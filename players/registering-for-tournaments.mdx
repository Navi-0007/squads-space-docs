## Finding the Register Button

From any tournament page, look for the Join button in the hero banner. The button shows different labels depending on the situation — "Register", "Join", or "Apply" for approval-required tournaments.

If the tournament requires an invite code (Private tournaments), you'll need one from the organizer to proceed.

## Registration Flow

### Solo Tournaments

For Solo format, registration is straightforward — you register as an individual player. No team selection needed.

### Duo and Squad Tournaments

For team formats, you'll choose how to register:

**Global Squad** — register with your existing squad. Your squad roster is used as-is. This is the fastest option if your team is already set up.

**Custom Members** — build a custom team for this specific tournament. You pick a squad name and select which members to include. Useful when you want a different lineup than your permanent squad.

After choosing, you may need to select specific members (for Custom) or confirm your roster.

### Invite Code

For Private tournaments (Invite Only or Invite Only with Approval), enter the invite code the organizer shared with you. Without it, you can't register.

### Club Membership

If the organizer requires club membership, you may be prompted to join their club during registration. This is a one-click action right in the registration flow.

## After Registering

What happens next depends on the registration mode:

- **Open (Public)** — you're confirmed immediately
- **Approval Required** — your registration goes to Pending status. The organizer reviews and approves or rejects it.
- **Invite Only** — confirmed immediately (you already had an invite)
- **Invite Only with Approval** — goes to Pending for organizer review

After registering, you'll see a success screen with next steps — like inviting teammates, providing your UID/IGN, or waiting for approval.

You'll also get a registration invite link to share with teammates who need to join your team.

## Invited Slots

If an organizer gives you an invited slot link for a later stage (like semifinals or finals), you can register through that link directly. Invited slot registration bypasses the normal registration flow — you join the specific stage the organizer designated.
