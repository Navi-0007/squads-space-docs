Clubs are the identity of tournament organizers on SquadsSpace. Every organizer has a club, and every tournament is associated with one. As a player, clubs help you find organizers, follow their events, and become part of their community.

## Browsing Clubs

The Clubs directory shows all public clubs on the platform. Search by name to find a specific organizer.

From the directory, you can also view your joined clubs — every club you're already a member of.

## Club Pages

Each club has a public page showing:

- **Banner and identity** — the club's name, region, and banner image
- **Stats** — member count and tournament count
- **Social links** — Discord, YouTube, WhatsApp links to connect with the community
- **Tournaments** — all tournaments and scrims run by this club, with status indicators
- **Members** — the club's member list

## Joining a Club

You can join a club in several ways:

- **From the club page** — click Join on any club's public page
- **During tournament registration** — if the organizer requires club membership, you'll be prompted to join during the registration flow
- **From a tournament page** — if a tournament has a club membership requirement, a join banner appears

Joining is free and instant. You can be a member of multiple clubs.

## Leaving a Club

From the club page, you can leave any club you've joined. If you have active tournament registrations with that club, you'll see a warning about which registrations might be affected.
